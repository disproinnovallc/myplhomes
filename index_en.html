Find the home of your dreams with PL Homes

Looking for a high‑quality manufactured, modular or mobile home? PL Homes offers one of the largest selections of single‑wide, double‑wide and tiny homes in Texas and Oklahoma, with sales centers in cities like Abilene, Austin, Elmendorf, Georgetown, Midland, New Braunfels, Oklahoma City, Odessa, San Antonio, San Antonio Clearance Center, Victoria and Willis. We offer new, used, move‑in ready and clearance homes to suit any budget. We also accept trade‑ins on your current home. In addition, we work with various manufactured home communities in Texas and Oklahoma to help you find the perfect location.

Need workforce housing solutions? We provide man camps and base camps, along with assistance in land improvements for a smooth installation. Call us at 855‑550‑6550 or visit us today to find your ideal home at the best price.

How it works

Start your purchase process by scheduling an appointment with one of our professional consultants. We’ll help you analyse your credit, explore financing options and choose among our available models to find the home that best fits your needs. PL Homes houses are built with the same materials and quality codes as traditional homes, but 80% of the process is completed in a factory, reducing time and cost.

  • Quality materials and specialised craftsmanship.
  • Fast construction process: fabrication in two weeks and delivery in 30‑90 days.
  • Financing assistance and the possibility to accept your current home as part of the payment.
